Communication campaign for the 2014 New Year's greetings of the City of&nbsp;Bobigny.
<br /><br />
We’ve been asked to work on many different formats following the catch phrase «Bobigny, gueule d’amour belle d’esprit». We took this chance to design a lot of different pictures in a generous mood. Each illustration is based on a limited vocabulary of simple shapes, trichromatic printed with bright colors.
<br /><br />
This project was made in collaboration with [Pierre Tandille](http://www.lecollecteur.fr).
